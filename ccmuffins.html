ccmuffins.html Chocolate Chip Muffins

These muffins are my very own recipe and they have no sugar, no butter, and are very healthy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • semisweet chocolate chips(measure with heart)

Preheat oven to 350. Line a muffin tin with muffin liners and spray with cooking spray.

Combine 1 cup oats and 1 cup skim milk and let those soak while you prepare the rest of the recipe, to soften the oats.

In a large bowl, whisk together the egg and mashed banana until combined.

Add 2 teaspoons vanilla and whisk to combine.

Add 1 cup flour, oat-milk mixture, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon. Stir until well combined but don't over mix.

Fold in chocolate chips.

Distribute the batter between the muffin tins - they will be about ⅔rds full.

Bake for 20 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean
