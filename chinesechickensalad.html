Chinese Chicken Salad a popular American-Asian fusion salad featuring shredded chicken, fresh greens, and crunchy toppings like wonton strips or almonds. It is typically tossed in a savory sesame-ginger vinaigrette, and is known for its sweet, savory, and crunchy profile.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Serves: 4

Cook time: 10-15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 4 cups shredded iceberg or romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded red/napa cabbage
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 1/2 cup fried rice noodles
  • 1/4 cup toasted sliced almonds

Prep: Combine shredded chicken, lettuce, cabbage, carrots, green onions, and almond in a large bowl.

Dress: Dress with storebought (sorry!) Chinese Chicken Salad Dressing.

Combine: Toss the salad with dressing just before serving.

Finish: Top with crispy wonton strips or noodles to maintain crunch.
