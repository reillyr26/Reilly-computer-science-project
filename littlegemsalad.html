Little Gem Salad is a crisp, sweet, and elegant salad featuring tender-yet-crunchy Little Gem lettuce, known for their sweet, buttery crunch,tossed in a creamy vinaigrette.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Serves: 4

Total time: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 medium heads little gem lettuce
  • 2 tablespoons champange vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped shallot
  • 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Instuctions

Cut stems from lettuce heads and remove any wilted outer leaves. Separate leaves and add to a large bowl of cold water. Lift leaves out of the water and transfer to a salad spinner or clean kitchen towel; spin or pat dry. Transfer clean, dry leaves to large salad bowl.

Step 2: In a medium bowl, whisk together vinegar, mustard, honey and shallot until combined. Add oil in a slow, steady stream, whisking constantly until combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 3: Drizzle dressing over little gem leaves and toss with clean hands or salad servers until evenly coated. Arrange dressed leaves on a large serving platter or divide between 4 individual salad plates. Sprinkle with chopped walnuts and serve.
