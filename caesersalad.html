Caeser Salad

Caeser Salad is a crisp, savory salad consisting of romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, and Parmesan cheese tossed in a creamy, tangy dressing.

Prep Time: 15 mins

Serves: 4

Cook time: 10-15 mins

Ingredients

  • For the Croutons
    • sourdough
    • grated garlic
    • olive oil
    • parmesan cheese
  • For the Dressing
    • lemon juice
    • anchovies
    • egg yolks
    • garlic
    • worcestershire sauce
    • dijon mustard
    • pepper
  • For the Salad
    • chopped romaine lettuce
    • parmesan
    • finished dressing
    • finished croutons

Step 1: Make the croutons

Tear 3 cups of sourdough into rough 1/2-inch pieces. Toss with 2 cloves grated garlic, 3 tablespoons olive oil, and a little grated Parmesan cheese. Bake until golden-brown and crispy.

Step 2: Make the dressing

Combine a tablespoon lemon juice,anchovies, 2 large egg yolks, 1 large clove minced garlic, 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, 3/4 tablespoon Dijon mustard, and pepper in a blender. With the blender running on high speed, slowly stream in 1/2 cup olive oil until thick and creamy. Transfer to a bowl and stir in some Parmesan.

Step 3: Assemble the Salad

In a large bowl, combine chopped romaine lettuce, Parmesan, dressing, and half of the croutons. Toss to evenly coat. Garnish with remaining croutons and more Parmesan.
